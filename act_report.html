Analysis on We Rate Dogs¶

Summary¶

After cleaning the data in our 3 tables, we are ready to perform analysis on the data we got from the We Rate Dogs twitter archive. As a frame of reference, this twitter account allows people to share photos of their dogs in order to be rated on the scale of their awesomness. Because no dog is ever lame, they are all rated beyond 100%. I concentrated my analysis efforts on the data that was fetched using the Tweepy API, along with the image predictions provided by Udacity's neural network.

  1. I then set out to analyze if there is a patter between the amout of times a tweet is favorited and retweeted.

As you would normally expect, with the exception of a few outliers, if a person favorites a tweet they are very likely to retweet it.
